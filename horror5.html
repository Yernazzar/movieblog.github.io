Psycho

★ 8.5/10

Horror, Mystery, Thriller

Marion Crane is unhappy with an affair with a divorced man who pays attention to her only on short visits. Therefore, the girl steals a large amount at work and in a hurry runs out of the city. Marion stops for the night at a motel run by Norman Bates, a young man suffering from the oppression of his beloved but domineering mother.

  • Director:Alfred Hitchcock
  • Writer:Joseph Stefano, Robert Bloch
  • Stars:Anthony Perkins • Janet Leigh • Vera Miles
  • Release Date: 1960
  • Runtime: 109 min
