Forrest Gump

★ 8.9/10

Drama, Comedy, History

Sitting at the bus stop, Forrest Gump — not very smart, but a kind and open guy — tells random people the story of his extraordinary life. Since he was very young, the guy suffered from a leg disease, the neighborhood boys teased him, but one day Forrest discovered his incredible ability to run. His childhood friend Jenny always supported and protected him, but soon their paths diverged.
