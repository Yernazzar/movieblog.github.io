Mr.Bean

★ 7.3/10

Comedy, Adventures

Mr. Bean works as a caretaker at the Royal British Gallery. The bosses would have fired him long ago according to the wording "for sleeping in the workplace", if not for the patronage of the director. Instead, Bean is sent on a business trip to Los Angeles. There he is mistaken for an official who came to the opening of the greatest exhibition in the USA...

  • Director:Mel Smith
  • Writer:Richard Curtis, Robin Driscoll
  • Stars:Rowan Atkinson • Peter MacNicol • Pamela Reed • Harris Yulin
  • Release Date: 1997
  • Runtime: 90 min
