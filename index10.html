Fight Club

★ 8.7/10

Thriller, Drama, Crime

An employee of an insurance company suffers from chronic insomnia and is desperately trying to escape from an excruciatingly boring life. One day, on another business trip, he meets a certain Tyler Durden, a charismatic soap merchant with a perverted philosophy. Tyler is sure that self—improvement is the lot of the weak, and the only thing worth living for is self—destruction.
