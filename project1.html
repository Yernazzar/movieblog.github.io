The Departed

★ 8.5/10

Crime, Drama, Thriller

The two best graduates of the police academy ended up on opposite sides of the barricade: one of them is a mafia agent in the ranks of law enforcement agencies, the other is a “mole” embedded in the mafia. Everyone considers it their duty to detect and destroy the enemy, but constant life in distorted realities changes the inner world of the heroes.

  • Director:Martin Scorsese
  • Writer:William Monahan, Alan Mak, Felix Chong
  • Stars:Leonardo DiCaprio • Matt Damon • Jack Nicholson
  • Release Date: 2006
  • Runtime: 151 min
